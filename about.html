About Interiar

Philosophy

Designing Spaces that Tell Stories

"A space should not only appeal to the eye. It must respond to the touch, the light, the acoustic envelope, and the soul."

Founded in 2014, Interiar began as a boutique architectural collective with a clear mandate: to reject standard mass-produced interiors in favor of highly customized, artisanal environments. Over the decade, our team has expanded into a full-service architectural studio capable of orchestrating complex residential renovations and commercial masterplans globally.

We work closely with local stonemasons, carpenters, and visual artists to ensure that every texture and detail in our designs has a purpose and is crafted to survive generations. Our approach is grounded in environmental integration—aligning interior layouts with natural wind corridors and light vectors to reduce energy consumption while maximizing human comfort.

Our Design Methodology

01

Concept & Visual Synthesis

We begin by extracting your design aspirations, material preferences, and functional needs to build mood boards, material palettes, and architectural drafts.

02

Detailing & 3D Drafting

Using laser-precise space planning and structural blueprints, we render 3D visualizations to experience the layout before laying a single brick.

03

Execution & Craft Curation

Our team oversees the builder procurement, material sourcing, site execution, and final furniture curation to ensure a flawless transfer of designs to reality.
